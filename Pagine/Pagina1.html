WHO I AM

I am a young amateur photographer, I live in Portogruaro
a small town in the province of Venice. I am 24 years old.
Since I was a child I have always traveled during the summer and winter vacation,
I have always seen new places and I have seen the old ones change.
Now I begin to document with my own eyes what I see from
like this document the changes that surround me especially for women future generations

WHAT I BRING

In my project I try to bring unique contents,
I bring photographs of natural landscapes, countryside or villages and towns
according to my point of view and trying to create different contents from each other

EQUIPMENT

For the shots I propose I use the Nikon D355
is an excellent camera to approach photography, the body
machine is handy and not bulky.
As a lens I use an 18-55mm, useful if you want to have a
general overview of what is in front of you, it is also useful for those who
he wants to do portrait photography of people, animals and plants.

ACCESSORIES

The accessories I'm using are 3 "K&F CONCEPT" brand filters and are the following:
UV filter
Polarizing Filter
ND4 filter -The UV filter is a filter that reduces the haze of the lens
-The filter Polarizing serves to reduce the reflected light
-The ND4 filter consists of a gray glass which reduces the exposure time and the diaphragm
